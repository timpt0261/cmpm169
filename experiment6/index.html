Experiment 6 - Subject/Topic

Imitate

I originally wanted to imitate a snake-like pattern that utilizes text to create a visibly interesting pattern. As well as taking inspiration from the Matrix to create a new visually appealing effect.

Integrate

With Grammars and Text Art being the grand focus I wanted to create the pattern through text. I integrated a class-labeled character grid to manage where to place text characters on the screen and manage variations with use to avoid the error of updating all characters on the screen simultaneously.

Innovate

To innovate I wanted to integrate the random Walk algorithm from the former program to create a more generative piece of art where the letters would shift around the canvas randomly.

Reflection

Reflecting on my progress, I am very glad to have been able to create something unique. While not my original intention I am glad how it turned out where the result is that of a dark tower in the distance floating above the water.
