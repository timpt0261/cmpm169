Experiment 3 - Generative Art

Imitate

I wanted to create an art piece that originally took inspiration from the Salvadoran Folk Artist Fernando Llort. Originally the idea was to create a generative piece of art that uses his use of geometric shapes and an intense color palette to create something alike.

Integrate

Keeping in mind the idea of generative art I wanted to integrate a procedural generation a background made of varying organic-looking shapes overlapping one another. However, I decided instead to create a simple implementation of a Random Walk Algorithm that would create points all around the canvas.

Innovate

To innovate I used code from my previous project to instead create circle rings, that would randomly be a color pallet from Fernando Llort`s work. As well as using noise and mapping depending on the mouse position it will create more sharper to smooth circles.

Reflection

Having worked on it by myself, I think I have worked on it for a good amount of time as though I didn't come to my original vision, I am still proud of it. As it still incorporates a lot of style.
