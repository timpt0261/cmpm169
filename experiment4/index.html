Experiment 4 - Images, Video, & Sound Art

Imitate

I originally wanted to imitate the visual appeal of a CRT TV or a GameBoy Color Display. I originally wanted to create something with a retro aesthetic.

Integrate

Keeping in mind of video specifically, I want to use P5.js's createVideo to be in real time. So I specifically tried to find a way to modify or change the video asestic. Especially how to render the pixels with varying shapes from squares, to circles, to points, to current lines.

Innovate

To innovate, I like to make all my pieces interactive so I add functionality so that the lines move around based on the mouse position. As well users can modify the number of lines and modify their width. As well as to change the shapes that are drawn throughout or to randomize it.

Reflection

I am comfortable with what I have accomplished because I am visually pleased with what I have created. Aging, though it was not what I originally intended to make I am satisfied with the overall project.
