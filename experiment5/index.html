Experiment 5 - 3D Graphics

Imitate

I originally wanted to imitate in creating a low poly scene of something mundane in everyday life. Originally, it was a low poly scene of a lighthouse near the ocean. However, when experimenting with the base and using simple geometric shapes I started to think of the Brutalism Architecture and Dark Scifi being incorporated into the scene.

Integrate

With 3d graphics in mind, I started to integrate techniques from Brutilism in creating large geometric buildings. While also integrating lighting techniques to create dark and ominous atmospheres.

Innovate

To innovate I wanted to add not only interactions but also lighting and textures. So I decided to start looking for images to create a more low poly in my scene. As well as implement a simple directional spotlight that follows the mouse around to simulate daylight and fog effect.

Reflection

Reflecting on my progress, I am very glad to have been able to create something more horror-based. While not my original intention I am glad how it turned out where the result is that of a dark tower in the distance floating above the water.
